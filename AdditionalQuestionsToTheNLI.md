### Description
The following questions represent fragments of the prompts used to evaluate the SPARQL-NLI system during preliminary testing. Their content is largely reflected in the final set of questions reported in the main paper; however, these fragments were employed in earlier evaluation stages and are provided here for the sake of transparency and completeness.

### Questions
- What are the part-level test keys run on parts of part-type 'mdsim_PX1'?
- Count the number of process runs for each distinct parameter recorded for parts of part-type mdsim_PX2
- What is the duration of each process run on Wafer W0PXA?
- Find all parts produced next to x = 1, y = 1, waferId = "W3PXA"
- Show all properties of a part-level test run
- Show all distinct process parameters and their unit for part-type 'mdsim_PX1'
- What processes is running directly after measuring parameter 'PXF_FLW_ADJ'
- Retrieve the part-level test results (pass/fail) of the part at (-1,2) of W0PXA
- What coordinates has the part located at the center of wafer W3PXA?
- List all parts produced next to the part at coordinates x = 1, y = 1 on the wafer with id "W3PXA"
